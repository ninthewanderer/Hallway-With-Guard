  ──── .✦ 🐀 ✦. ──── <br><br>
  <i> Rot & Prowl is a 3D stealth survival game where you play as a mouse trying to avoid death by the claws of a possessed cat.<br>
  Sneak through the wizard's abandoned house, stay out of sight, and survive long enough to escape with your cheese. <br> </i>

  <p align="center"> 
    This game was required to be first-person, 3D, and made in Unity. Additionally, NavMesh had to be utilized for the enemy AI. <br>
    The player's core objective is to escape a guard protecting a MacGuffin located at the end of a hallway. <br>
    There needed to be 3 inter-connected areas, one of which being the "hallway" being guarded. <br>
    Additionally, 3D assets needed to be modularly designed. <br>
